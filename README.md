# 📊 Mobile Shop Sales Analysis

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing real-world sales data from a mobile retail shop to uncover key business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The goal is to identify sales patterns, customer behavior, and the impact of offers and pricing strategies on overall business performance.

---

## ❓ Business Questions

- Which mobile brands and products generate the highest revenue?
- What is the impact of different offers/discounts on sales performance?
- What are the peak sales days and time periods?
- How do customer ratings vary across products and offers?
- Which factors influence higher sales — price, offers, or timing?
- How can the store optimize inventory and marketing strategies?

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

- Source: Real-world sales data from a local mobile shop
- Data includes:
  - Product details
  - Sales transactions
  - Pricing and offers
  - Date & time of purchase
  - Customer ratings

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📈 Certain brands/products contribute a major portion of total revenue.
- 🎯 Discount offers significantly boost sales compared to regular pricing.
- 🕒 Sales peak during evening hours, indicating high customer activity.
- 📅 Weekends and special periods show higher transaction volumes.
- ⭐ Higher-rated products tend to perform more consistently.
- 💰 Mid-range products generate higher sales frequency than premium ones.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- 📦 **Inventory Optimization:**  
  Increase stock for top-selling products to avoid missed sales opportunities.

- 🎯 **Promotional Strategy:**  
  Run targeted discount campaigns and festive offers to boost revenue.

- 🕒 **Peak Hour Management:**  
  Ensure sufficient staff and stock availability during high-demand hours.

- ⭐ **Customer Experience Improvement:**  
  Address issues in low-rated products/services to enhance satisfaction.

- 💰 **Pricing Strategy:**  
  Focus on mid-range products as they attract more customers.

- 📣 **Marketing Optimization:**  
  Align promotions with high-performing days and time slots.
